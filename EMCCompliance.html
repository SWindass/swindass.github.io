EMC Compliance

With the proliferation of high-speed digital circuitry, high-current switchmode power supplies and vairous forms of wireless connectivity, electromagnetic problems are now abundant for poorly designed PCBs.

ntronIQ design each PCB to achieve the best EMC performance. Preventing various forms of interference, including:

• Electromagnetic Interference (EMI)
• Conducted Emmissions
• Electromagnetic susceptability (EMS)

ntronIQ provide the requisite testing for compliance - ensuring the the correct certification is in place for each region where the product is to be sold.

We also offer a consultancy service to track down and fix existing EMC problems.
